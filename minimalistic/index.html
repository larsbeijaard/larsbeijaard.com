I am a Software Engineer, currently working at Sensiks. Sensiks is a company that enhances the VR experiences by using physical characteristics such as using 360° heaters to mimic the heat of the sun. Or using 360° fans to mimic the breeze of the beach going through your hair.

At the moment I study Game Development at Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht. I am in the fourth and final year of the study. After I get my degree I want to study Applied Mathematics, and with that knowledge start my BSc, followed by an MSc, in Computer Science.

Astronomy is also a big hobby of mine, I love everything about it; watching planets through my telescope, taking pictures of the stars, planets, and more. My passion for astronomy got a huge boost after I went to the Planetarium in Franeker, the Netherlands with my grandpa. The Planetarium is our entire solar system, as far as it was known back in 1774, recreated on a scale using pure mechanics. It was built by the astronomer and mathematician, Eise Eisinga.
