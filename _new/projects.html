HomeworkNotifyer

The Project The amount of assignments you get sometimes could be a bit overwhelming, so for that reason I created a Discord Bot. The bot has a simple concept. You assign the bot homework with the description of what you need to do exactly (and add reference links, etc.) and the deadline.
When the deadline is coming closer and closer, the bot will notify you that the deadline is coming closer and closer.
What have I learnt? I have learnt working inside the C# Discord API. I have also learnt how to setup, run and maintain a Discord bot.
Currently, the bot is being held up on my Raspberry PI.
